anachronorium: 
Anchronorium is the DarkPsy project of Portuguese producer Rafyx, exploring high-intensity soundscapes built on dissonant textures, immersive atmospheres, and fast, driving rhythms. His music blends detailed sound design with a cinematic sense of darkness, creating deep mental journeys and powerful nighttime energy on the dancefloor.
With a signature style marked by high BPMs, hypnotic storytelling and dense psychedelic layers, Anchronorium delivers a modern and impactful interpretation of the dark psychedelic spectrum. The project continues to evolve with new releases and performances, establishing a strong and distinctive presence within contemporary DarkPsy.

Dr Fractal :
Dr Fractal is a psytrance project that has evolved over the years through different musical paths, both within electronic music and beyond. A musician since childhood and a drummer in different bands, he gradually discovered the wide spectrum of electronic genres, moving through dub, d’n’b, techno, and falling in love with psytrance. His music stands out because he’s always chosen to swim against the tide, drawing inspiration from what he listens to without ever trying to replicate it. Instead, he’s developed a personal sound marked by deep and immersive atmospheres, sharp and powerful beats, and a sought-after sound design. The result is a rich and evocative sonic identity.
Rather than following trends or fitting into predefined molds, Dr Fractal aims above all to offer a unique auditory experience—one that resonates both physically and emotionally

La Marquise :
La Marquise, or Angélo, has been creating music for over fifteen years.
He discovered psytrance in 2012 and immediately fell in love with the genre, drawn above all by its textures and sonic richness. His style sits somewhere between groovy night psy and slightly darker atmospheres, while maintaining a playful, mischievous and driving energy. La Marquise crafts live sets where movement, sonic substance and surprise are at the heart of the experience. In 2019, he joined the Transubtil Records label. His first EP, Precisely Nowhere, was released in 2023. He also recorded two sets for Radiozora, in 2020 and 2022, and has contributed to numerous compilations over the years.
La Marquise is a constantly evolving project, driven by a passion for sound and a desire to take listeners on a journey.

Opsuruus:
Sandro Viegas from Portugal, is the creative mind behind Opsuruus, one of the rising names in the new generation of portuguese darkpsy and darkforest artists.
His music unfolds through unpredictable moments, always guided by a core concept:  contrast.
Contrast of feelings, atmospheres and sonic textures.
He is known for delivering infections energy on the dancefloor, blending hypnotic drive with bursts of pure, controlled insanity that takes the audience on an intense and vibrant journey.
Sandro had the privilege of learning from artists such as Grub, Rawar, Ulvae, Gandhabba who played an important role throughout his artistic path.
With focus, dedication and an increasingly defined identity, Opsuruus promises a path full of evolution and power.

Xipe Totecs:
Xipe totecs is the Psytrance project from Japan, who started in 2018.
They decided to keep exploring music from the"rave"scene, producing powerful sounds.
Their universe encompasses epic themes and colorful frenzied drums, to create trance music full of new sororities. 
In 2020, they signed to the French label Transubtil records

They released  their first EP ‘Evil in the shell’ in June 2022 which reached number 1 in several music charts. In the same year they started touring in Mexico, Brazil and Europe. Overcoming the global pandemic, the band has been active in Japan and abroad
